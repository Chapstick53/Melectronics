Our Story

Founded in 2009, Prestige began as a small electrical trading company with a simple mission: to provide high-quality electrical products at competetive prices. Over the years, we have grown to becomeone of the india’s most trusted wholesale dealers in the electrical industry.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, strategic partnerships with leading brands, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Today, we serve thousands of customers across India, from small electrical contractors to large industrial enterprises.
